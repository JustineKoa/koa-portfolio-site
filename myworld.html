MY WORLD



Dublin High Graduate || Freshman at UC Irvine

My interest in coding branched off of my original love for video games like Harvest Moon, the Sims, and Kingdom Hearts. Ever since I attended Kode with Klossy 2016 in LA, I've been eager to keep learning more and expand my horizons! I was inspired by my classmates during the course to continue building off the foundation we created together. I've realized that our world today is full of code, and I want to make it mine. I want to share my creations with the world and help empower other women in the tech field around me. I'm driven, intelligent, ambitious, and perserverant. As long as I keep chasing my dreams, my world has no limits.
