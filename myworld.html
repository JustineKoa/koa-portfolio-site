ABOUT ME

My interest in coding branched off of my original love for video games like Harvest Moon, the Sims, and Kingdom Hearts. Ever since I attended Kode with Klossy 2016 in LA, I've been eager to keep learning more and expand my horizons! I was inspired by my classmates during the course to continue building off the foundation we created together. I've realized that our world today is full of code, and I want to make it mine. I want to share my creations with the world and help empower other women in the tech field around me. I'm driven, intelligent, ambitious, and perserverant. As long as I keep chasing my dreams, my world has no limits! Aside from my interest for computer science, I am also an avid watcher of the Walking Dead and I'm a huge fan of musicals. As you could tell from my photos, I am a firm believer that Disneyland is indeed the happiest place on Earth! I love venturing to new places to try different food, but I also enjoy staying home to spend time with family and my sister's two adorable corgis!
